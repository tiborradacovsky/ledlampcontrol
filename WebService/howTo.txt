Lamp details are available via rest service. To obtain details for lamp #2 you need to do GET request http://localhost:8080/lamps/2

Supported GET requests:
http://localhost:8080/lamps
http://localhost:8080/lamps/{lampID}

To obtain details for all lamps:
http://localhost:8080/lamps
To obtain details for 3rd lamp:
http://localhost:8080/lamps/3
